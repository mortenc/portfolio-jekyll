About

Hi, I'm Morten

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer who thrives in the intersection of strategy, user experience, user interface design and front-end development.

I currently live in San Francisco, where I lead a team designers as US Creative Director for FFW Agency. I was born in Copenhagen, Denmark, and raised on a diet of Nordic minimalism and functional aesthetics.

On an average week I wear many hats; from running workshops and interviews, to tackling complex information architecture, and pushing pixels, tweaking typography and animating micro interactions.
