About me

Hi, I'm Morten. I'm a multidisciplinary designer who thrives in the intersection of strategy, user experience, user interface design and front-end development.

I currently live in San Francisco, California, where I lead a team of designers as US Creative Director for FFW Agency. I was born in Copenhagen, Denmark, and raised on a diet of Scandinavian minimalism and functional aesthetics.

When I'm not running workshops with clients, interviewing users, or tackling complex information architecture, you'll find me heads down in front of my screen; pushing pixels, tweaking typography or coding complex animations.
